Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?
While we highly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time and therapist, we pride ourselves on flexibility and can often accommodate walk-ins or same-day appointments within the hour.
What should I wear to my massage?
Dress comfortably. During the massage, you will be draped with a sheet or towel at all times, and only the area being worked on will be exposed. You may undress to your level of comfort.
Do you offer specialized post-surgery massages?
Yes, we specialize in after-lipo care and lymphatic drainage massages. Our therapists are trained to provide gentle, effective treatments to aid in your recovery process.
Can I request a male or female therapist?
Absolutely. We have a diverse team of both male and female professional therapists. Please mention your preference when booking your appointment.
